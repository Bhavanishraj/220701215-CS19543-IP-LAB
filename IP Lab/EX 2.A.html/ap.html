AndhraPradesh

  • Andhra Pradesh is the birthplace of Kuchipudi, one of the eight classical dance forms of India.

  • The Tirumala Venkateswara Temple in Tirupati is one of the most famous pilgrimage sites in India, attracting millions of devotees every year.

  • Major ports like Visakhapatnam, one of the largest ports in India, and the new deep-water port at Krishnapatnam contribute significantly to the state's economy through maritime trade and shipbuilding.

  • Andhra Pradesh is Known as the "Rice Bowl of India," Andhra Pradesh is a major producer of rice, along with other crops like tobacco, cotton, and chili peppers. The state is also famous for its horticultural production.

  • Additionally, the development of Amaravati as the state's new capital is aimed at creating a modern, smart city with advanced infrastructure and sustainable development.
